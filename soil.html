Soil Condition Identifier 🌳

✨💚 Instructions

  • Cut a small strip of paper (any paper works).
  • Color the bottom edge with a marker.
  • Press the colored edge gently into the soil for 3–5 seconds.
  • Take a clear photo of the paper strip.
  • Upload the photo below.

🌈 What the Colors Mean

🌿 Perfect Condition — soil moisture is ideal
💧 Needs More Water — soil is too dry
🌊 Overwatered — soil is too wet
🪴 Root Rot Risk — poor drainage or waterlogged
🌫️ Soil Compaction — roots may struggle to grow
🍂 Low Nutrients — soil may need fertilizing
☀️ Too Much Sun — soil dries too quickly
🌧️ Too Little Sun — soil stays cold and damp
