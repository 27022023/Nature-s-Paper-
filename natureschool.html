Plant Encyclopedia 🌿

🌱 Tropical Plants
Bright indirect light, humidity 60–80%, compost-rich soil, eco-friendly misting.
🌵 Succulents & Cacti
Full sun, gritty soil, water every 2–3 weeks, terracotta pots.
🌿 Herbs
Full sun, moist soil, trim often, reduce food waste.
🌸 Flowering Plants
High light, monthly fertilizer, remove spent blooms.
🌳 Trees & Shrubs
Deep watering, seasonal pruning, mulch for root protection.
🪴 Vines
Medium light, easy propagation, eco-friendly supports.
🪰 Carnivorous Plants
Rainwater only, high humidity, nutrient-poor soil.
🌊 Aquatic Plants
Clean water, moderate light, natural pond bacteria.
🌿 Ferns
Low light, high humidity, moist soil.
🌾 Grasses & Bamboo
Fast-growing, renewable, bright light.
🌱 Mosses
Low light, constant moisture, terrarium-friendly.
